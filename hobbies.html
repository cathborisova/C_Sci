Hobbies

I am a cross country runner, a nordic skier and
a photographer. I started running because my dad
ran marathons and my mom did track and feild so
they pushed me to start running. At first I would
run a mile or two every now and then but once I got
onto the team I really started enjoying it and have
been running a lot more. I got into skiing the same
way, my dad was a nordic skier and also advised me
to join. At first I didn't like it because I thought
it was really hard, but once I got into the hang of it
I fell in love with the sport.I joined both sports in
8th grade, but before that I was a rhythmic gymnast,
but ended up quitting because I couldn't keep up with
school and the sport. I started photography in the
beginning of 2016, after I started taking pictures
on my family's vacation in Florida.
