Skittles Lab

Are all mini skittle packets created equally?

Conclusion: Not all mini skittle packets are created equally.

Our data shows that not a single one of the skittle packets were the same.
-The average mass of the skittle packets was 15.29g, but the masses ranged from 10.3g to 18.4g.
-The average amount of skittles in each of the packets was 14.8, but ranged from 10 to 18.
-The average amount of reds in each packet was 3.3, but the amount ranged from 1 to 7, out of 33 total.
-The average amount of purples in each packet was 2.5, but the amount ranged from 1 to 4 out of the 25 total.
-The average amount of oranges in each packet was 2.9, but the amount ranged from 1 to 6 out of the 29 total.
-The average amount of yellows in each packet was 3.3, but the amount ranged from 1 to 6 out of the 33 total.
-The average amount of greens in each packet was 2.8, but the amount ranged from 1 to 5 out of the 28 total.
